Property la khai niem dat biet (doi khi con duoc goi la contro), 
- tao ra 1 mask bao ve cac private fields ben trong class
- gom 1 bo getter va setter:
	getter : lay gia tri cua private field (return)
	setter : gan gia tri cua property (value - sau khi dc kiem tra) cho private field
- co cung kieu du lieu voi private field
- co 3 loai:
	read-write : getter va setter
	read-only : getter
	write-only : setter
